Try the app

Get a ticket as a client and try to serve it as an operator. Or manage the queue as a supervisor.

(For convenience, open different roles in different browser tabs)

Client

  • Chooses a reason of a visit
  • Gets a ticket
  • Watches the screen until the ticket is called
Go as Client

Operator

  • Logins to serve tasks
  • Receives tickets in order & according to task priority
  • Can redirect a ticket to another operator
  • Can serve clients who missed the turn
Go as Operator

Supervisor

  • Starts / ends a working day
  • Creates new operators and tasks
  • Monitors all queues
Go as Supervisor
